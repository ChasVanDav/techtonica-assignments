Vanessa's Kimchi Recipe

Kimchi, or spicy fermented cabbage, is a food staple in every Korean kitchen. Just before the grueling winter months, Koreans gather as a family and make large batches of Kimchi that last until the spring. This gathering is called 'Kimjang,' a tradition passed down for a thousand years. Similar to saurkraut or pickles, kimchi is a great tangy accompaniment to any heavy or greasy meal. Since it is a fermented food, it takes some time to prepare. However, it's well worth the wait and I guarantee you'll be making it time and time again.
